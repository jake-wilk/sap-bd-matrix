Notes & Data Caveats

  • BD Window is calculated from March 2026 based on the sooner of: next API 653 internal inspection (5-10 year cycle) or next cleaning turnaround (2-4 year cycle). Where no confirmed date is available, last known inspection/construction date plus typical cycle is used. Verify before outreach.
  • API 653 governs aboveground steel storage tanks. Internal inspection frequency is risk-based (RBI): typically 5 years for highly corrosive products (concentrated H2SO4, HCl), up to 10 years for less corrosive products with strong RBI data. New tanks (post-construction) require first internal inspection within 10 years.
  • Hazardous chemical tanks (Class 8 corrosives, Class 6.1 toxics) require specialist contractors with HazMat entry certification, neutralisation capability, and confined space competency. This is APMS's core value proposition in this market.
